Welcome to Crooks

With a firm belief in quality, award-winning chefs creating award-winning meals, and our unique beverages, Crooks is an experience like no other in Novi. With an environment focused around comfort and fun, and with a team that wants to make you feel at home, Crooks is a great place to enjoy good times with good people and good food.

Our Mission

Since the day Crooks opened in 1992, we have consistently strived to give the best experience possible for our customers. This is reflected in everything we do, from the food we make, to the environment we create. We want you to be comfortable at Crooks, and we work every day to make that reality.

Our Food

For the last 3 decades, Crooks has been making award-winning meals such as our USDA-certified Sirloin Steaks, and our menu has evolved with new additions every year, from our Perfectly Pulled Pork, to our signature Pretzel Dogs. Crooks is commited to making fantastic food for you to enjoy.

Our Drinks

Our Bartenders offer world-acclaimed beverages including well known brands loved by many as well as Crook Signatures, including our best-selling Pink Margarita and non-alchoholic Strawberry Lemonade.
