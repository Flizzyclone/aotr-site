Advanced Placement in the World Language Department

Advanced Placement Spanish Language:

Prerequisite: Spanish 4 or Equivalent Proficiency

Grade: 11-12

Class Length: 40 Weeks

This course builds upon the skills acquired in the Level 4 course and deals with communication; culture; understanding of, and participation in, local and international communities. This course includes the teaching of Advanced Placement material. Students are encouraged, but not required, to take the advanced placement exam for this course, which takes place in May. Students who have not completed level 4 should have the equivalent proficiency and should have completed LA 10 as this course requires advanced critical thinking, writing, reading, and grammar skills.
